About Srivenkateshwara NON Woven Bags

Our Story

Located in the heart of Kottur Shadnagar, Srivenkateshwara NON Woven Bags stands as a beacon of quality and sustainability in the packaging industry. Under the visionary leadership of our proprietors Jamuna and Narsimlu, we have established ourselves as a trusted name in non-woven bag manufacturing.

Our Mission

We are committed to providing eco-friendly, durable, and cost-effective packaging solutions that help businesses reduce their environmental footprint while maintaining the highest standards of quality and functionality.
